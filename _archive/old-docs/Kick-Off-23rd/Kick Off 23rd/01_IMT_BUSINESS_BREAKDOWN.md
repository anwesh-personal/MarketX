## 📌 WHAT IS INMARKET TRAFFIC?

InMarketTraffic (IMT) is a **B2B market activation system**.

**In Simple Terms:**
> IMT helps B2B companies reach people who are READY TO BUY right now, and they only pay when someone actually clicks (verified, not anonymous).

---

## 💡 THE IMT VALUE PROPOSITION

### What IMT IS:
- ✅ A way to reach your entire ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
- ✅ Email-driven conversations that lead to sales
- ✅ Pay only for verified, identity-linked clicks (CPC model)
- ✅ Full buyer record from every click

### What IMT IS NOT:
- ❌ An ad platform
- ❌ A list broker
- ❌ An email marketing tool
- ❌ An intent data reseller
- ❌ A lead vendor
- ❌ A replacement for sales teams

## 🎯 PRIMARY METRIC: BOOKED CALL

Everything in IMT's world optimizes for **BOOKED_CALL**.

Not opens. Not clicks. Not replies. **BOOKED CALLS.**

```
Hierarchy of Signals:

PRIMARY:     BOOKED_CALL (This is success)
             │
SECONDARY:   ├── REPLIES (Good signal)
             └── CLICKS (Okay signal)
             │
IGNORED:     └── OPEN_RATE (Unreliable, use REPLY_RATE as proxy)
             │
GUARDRAILS:  ├── BOUNCES (Bad - email health)
             ├── UNSUBSCRIBES (Bad - list health)
             └── COMPLAINTS (Very Bad - reputation)
```
